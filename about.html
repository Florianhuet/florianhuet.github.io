About me

Summary


Thrid year student at Epitech innovation school, video games development and mobile application enthusiast, at the head of a coding association that aims to let the young people discover the IT world of programming, my goal is to become an official part of the IT video game industry.

Game programmer in my spare time, i'm in love with video games and video game developement.
